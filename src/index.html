About Empire Partner Foundation

We are a non-profit organization that was established in 2016 with the sole purpose of empowering the youth to build a better South Africa and African continent through technology. At Empire Partner Foundation Tech Hub, we believe that technology can be used as a tool to solve some of the key socio-economic challenges that we face today.
