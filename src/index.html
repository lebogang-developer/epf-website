About Us

We are a non-profit organization that was established in 2016 with the sole purpose of empowering the youth to build a better South Africa and African continent through technology. At Empire Partner Foundation Tech Hub, we believe that technology can be used as a tool to solve some of the key socio-economic challenges that we face today.

Youth Pillar

Under the Youth pillar, we aim to provide young people with access to technology and digital skills training. This includes programs such as coding boot camps, hackathons, and mentorship opportunities to help the youth develop skills and realize their full potential.

Technology Pillar

The Technology pillar is focused on driving innovation and the adoption of emerging technologies in addressing 10 Key Sector Challenges. We believe that by leveraging technology, we can help solve some of the biggest challenges facing our continent and improve the quality of life for all.
